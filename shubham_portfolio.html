XparkAI — Hyperlocal Medical Logistics Platform

XparkAI is a dual-app Android platform (patient-facing + pharmacy partner) that leverages geofencing, Haversine-based proximity bidding, and a three-layer AI assistant powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash to connect patients with nearby pharmacies and healthcare resources in real time. Built on a hybrid Firebase Firestore + Supabase PostgreSQL architecture, XparkAI ensures fast, reliable data across the logistics chain — from medicine availability to emergency blood unit location and hospital bed tracking.

The platform operates within a 3.0 km geofence radius with a 10-minute broadcast window, ensuring hyper-relevant, low-latency matches. Co-developed as a six-member team at KIIT, under the guidance of Dr. Mainak Biswas, with a research paper targeting IEEE Access.
